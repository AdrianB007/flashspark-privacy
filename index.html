Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

FlashSpark ("we", "our", "the app") is a free vocabulary learning application. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the app handles, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Data we store on your device

All flashcard sets, folders, learning progress, and app preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data. It is never uploaded to our servers. If you uninstall the app, this data is permanently deleted.

2. Data sent to third-party services

When you use the AI Generate feature to create flashcard sets, the text you enter (such as a topic or prompt) is sent to an external AI service (API) for processing. This is necessary to generate flashcard content based on your request.

We do not store the text you send or the responses received. The data is transmitted solely for the purpose of generating flashcards and is not used for any other purpose. Please refer to the AI service provider's own privacy policy for details on how they handle data.

3. Data we do NOT collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

Personal information (name, email, phone number) · Account or login credentials · Location data · Device identifiers or advertising IDs · Usage analytics or crash reports · Photos, files, or voice recordings (these are processed entirely on your device)

4. Children's privacy

The app is suitable for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since the app does not collect personal data from any user, no special data handling for children is necessary.

5. Third-party services

The app does not include any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The only external communication is the AI text generation feature described in section 2.

6. Data security

Since all user data is stored locally on your device, security depends on your device's own security measures (screen lock, encryption). The AI feature uses encrypted (HTTPS) connections when transmitting data.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify users through the app or on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
